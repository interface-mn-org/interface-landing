## VISUAL GUIDELINES

**Hormozi Style Elements:**
- Bold, large headline (takes 60% of space)
- High contrast (dark bg + white/yellow text OR white bg + black text)
- Minimal design - no clutter
- One clear CTA
- Numbers are BIG when used
- Face/person optional but helps

**Brand Colors:**
- Black background + Orange/Yellow text (aggressive)
- White background + Black text (clean)
- Orange accent for CTA button (#FF6600)

**Formats:**
- Square (1:1) for IG feed
- Story (9:16) for IG/FB stories
- Landscape (1.91:1) for FB feed

---

## TESTING STRATEGY

1. **Start with 3-5 different angles** (Fear, Value, Urgency)
2. **Run each for 3-5 days** with $5-10/day budget
3. **Track CTR and conversions** per angle
4. **Scale winners**, kill losers
5. **Create variations** of winning angles
